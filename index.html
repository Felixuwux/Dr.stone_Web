Plot

It is a manga written and drawn by the mangaka Boichi, he since the age of 10 generated a love for drawing and art, then he decided to study physics to create manga with a science fiction theme.
One of the creations of this man is Dr.stone, this manga was published on March 6, 2017 in shonen jump magazine. The context of the manga basically is in the world in the 21st century when in the sky appears a light and a wave that pretifies all humans around the world, then in the year 5738 a scientist named senku wakes up and sets out to awaken all humans in the world with science
